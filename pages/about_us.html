About Us


The website aims to provide an interactive platform to interact with those students who have faced the company interview and can chat with the senior person according to their interest as one feels hesitation in a new atmosphere and as a output they are unable to interact with teachers or seniors about certain things. In addition, they will also get some more information which will help them to face interview with confidence and to overcome certain problems that are faced by a fresher as experience plays a major role in all field. So, we named it “GLA CONNECTIFY” which suggests to connect GLA students and management together on a single platform.

Motivation


In current scenario, student do not get exposure to the real life. In order of scoring high CPI, they just focus on the learning part and forgot the application of the concept in the real life. Now-a-days, Students mainly are placement oriented but they do not get the right guidelines regarding the placement drives, companies, need of the companies and technologies which are in trend. So, we come up with this idea of making a webpage for helping such student where they can find all the needed information at one place. The basic advantage of this project is that it is time saving as it saves the time of the student which was wasted in browsing the same information on different sites.
